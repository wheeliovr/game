# Privacy Policy for Weelio

**Last Updated:** [Date]

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application **Weelio** ("Application") for Meta Quest devices that was created by **Code 61**.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage (VRC.Privacy.2 & VRC.Privacy.3)

**Personal Data**
We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers. We do not require you to create an account to play the game.

**Gameplay Data**
The Application stores game progress locally on your device using standard storage methods (PlayerPrefs). This includes:
*   High scores
*   Level completion status
*   Settings preferences (Volume, Haptics)

This data is stored strictly on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

## 2. Third-Party Data Collection (VRC.Privacy.4)

The Application is built using the Unity Game Engine and runs on the Meta Quest platform. These third-party services may collect data as described in their own privacy policies:

*   **Unity Technologies:** May collect hardware and software information (e.g., device type, OS version) for analytics and error reporting.
    *   [Unity Privacy Policy](https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy)
*   **Meta Platforms, Inc.:** As this app runs on Meta Quest hardware, Meta collects platform-level data regarding app usage and performance.
    *   [Meta Privacy Policy](https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy/)

## 3. Data Retention and Deletion

Since we do not store your data on external servers, you have full control over your data. You can delete your game data at any time by uninstalling the Application from your device or clearing the app data through the system settings.

## 4. Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, or have questions about our practices, please contact us via email at **wheeliothegame@gmail.com**.
